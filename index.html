Company

Amazon

Amazon, founded by Jeff Bezos in 1994, started as an online bookstore but quickly expanded its horizons to become one of the world's largest and most influential technology companies. Headquartered in Seattle, Washington, Amazon has revolutionized the way people shop online, offering everything from books to electronics, clothing, and even groceries. Amazon’s growth can be attributed to its innovative approach to technology and customer service. The company introduced the concept of customer reviews and personalized recommendations, which have become standard in e-commerce.
